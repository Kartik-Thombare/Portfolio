Top Movies Of All Time According To Kartik

My top 3 movies of all-time

Spirited Away

My favorite movie of all time. It's a Japanese animated movie that is about a girl who gets lost in a spirit world. It's a very beautiful movie and I love the story and the characters.

Toy Story

My second favorite movie of all time. It's a Pixar movie about toys that come to life when people aren't around. It's a very funny movie and I love the characters.

Up

My third favorite movie of all time. It's a Pixar movie about an old man who goes on an adventure with a young boy. It's a very touching movie and I love the characters.
